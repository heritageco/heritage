Virtual Assistant

Remote Part-Time / Full-Time United States

We are looking for a highly organized and proactive Virtual Assistant to support the day-to-day operations of Shelly Heritage Archaeology. This is a remote position with flexible hours, ideal for someone who is detail-oriented, communicative, and eager to support meaningful cultural resource work.

You will work closely with our principal archaeologist, helping to manage communications, coordinate scheduling, support report preparation, and keep projects moving efficiently.

Responsibilities

  • Manage and organize email correspondence and respond to routine inquiries
  • Coordinate scheduling, meetings, and project timelines
  • Assist with formatting and proofreading technical reports and documents
  • Maintain organized digital filing systems for project records
  • Conduct background research and compile reference materials as needed
  • Support invoicing, client communications, and general administrative tasks
  • Assist with website updates, social media, or outreach as needed

Qualifications

  • Proven experience as a virtual or administrative assistant
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Proficiency with Google Workspace, Microsoft Office, or equivalent tools
  • Self-motivated and comfortable working independently and remotely
  • Interest in archaeology, cultural heritage, or the natural sciences is a plus
